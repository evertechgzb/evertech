Projects & Services

Evertech is involved in various Electrical projects of new Substations & Power Plants of private and public sector at domestic & international level.

Our services start from initial design, installation, detailed testing of Substation up to 765 KV till final commissioning of all electrical equipment like Transformers, Generators, Circuit Breakers, Isolators, CTs, PTs, HT\LT Panels etc.

Project Roles:

  • nstallation, Testing & Commissioning of all type of electrical equipment like Transformer, Generator Stator, Circuit Breaker, CT, PT, HT/LT Panel etc. in electrical substations from 415V to 765 KV
  • Testing of electrical equipment required for assessment of their healthiness & correct working.
  • Relay Testing - Testing of all relay generations electromechanical, static, numerical, using specialized CMC256 OMICRON relay test kit
  • Diagnostic Test on the insulation of cables & windings using Automatic High Voltage Capacitance & TAN DELTA Set (Omicron/ Meggar Make)
  • Transformer Testing & Verification of unusual events determined during testing using Sweep Frequency Response Analyzer (SFRA) kit
  • Testing of all types of CBs LV, MV, EHV, UHV using Circuit Breaker Dynamic Contact Resistance Meter (DCRM) Kit
  • Testing & Protective Coordination of Relay for power plant & power system study by ETAP software.
